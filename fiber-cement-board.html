Fiber Cement Board Manufacturing Process



Fiber Cement Board (VBoard) is a Non Asbestos and autoclaved product, manufactured by the Hatschek process followed curin using HPSC technology.The curing is done under high pressure steam to attain the product’s strength and stability. Vboard is composed of a composite matrix containing special grade cellulose fibres, ordinary Portland cement, fine silica, quartz, and some mineral additives.



Fibre Cement Board Applications


Strong as cement, better than wood.

Fiber cement boards have taken the international market by storm. The strong portfolio of advantages make the product widely endorsed and appreciated by architects and customers worldwide. Many of them are replacing plywood with fiber cement boards as they save time, money and they are aesthetically more beautiful in their appeal. Multiple uses of Vboard has taken fibre cement board utilities to the next level for use in interior as well as exterior applications.




Fibre Cement Board Advantages


Quick, affordable & durable construction.

Fiber cement board is strong and designed to hold up under extreme climates where intense sunlight, moisture or wind is common. Fiber cement sheet is also resistant to fire, water, insects, rotting and moisture. Fiber cement board is non-asbestos, flexible, tough and long lasting product.
